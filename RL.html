Rocket League

VIDERE's Rocket League team was formed in January of 2020 and it has been nothing but wins ever since.

CJCJ

Cameron "CJCJ" Johns (born August 6, 1998) is an Australian Rocket League player currently playing for VIDERE.

Express

Jonathan "Express" Slade (born August 22, 1998) is an Australian Rocket League player.

Drippay

Matthew "Drippay" Den-Kaat (born March 9, 2000) is an Australian Rocket League player, who lived in the United States while playing for Evil Geniuses. He has now moved back over to Oceania to compete with VIDERE.
